The Problem

The world's population is gradually growing, and the demand for water is increasing with it. For each person that passes every day, two more are born. If our numbers continue to grow this steadily,our inability to gain water will eventually become the cause of war and devastation. We need to find a solution, and fast. Not only scientists can help solve this global problem, everybody can, including kids.

Background

This website is built to inform people of all ages (especially children) about the water problem and let them contribute to the discovery of a solution. There is a reading list and a news page, as well as pages with experiments and brainstorming. On top of that, you can donate money to our website and other organizations on the fundraising page as well. You can find these in the menu at the top of the page.
