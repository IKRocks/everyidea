Fundraising

There are many different organizations and charities that focus on raising money to improve the living conditions in developing countries. Several of these do so by helping to provide clean water solutions at the level of a family or village.

5 Nonprofits that make clean water a global reality is a good place to start to learn more about some of these.

Charity:Water is a non-profit organization that makes it easy to donate. On the website, you can ask for donations instead of birthday gifts. By donating here, you help citizens of third-world countries get greatly-needed clean water.

There are also companies and funds that you can invest in that are working towards developing solutions for water scarcity. Forbes published an article in 2016 about what water stocks some investment gurus were investing in.
