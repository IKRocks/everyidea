Experiments - Basic Filter

  • Supplies:
    • gravel
    • sand
    • fine sand
    • a coffee filter
    • a plastic water bottle
    • scissors
    • rubber band
    • a glass of dirty water
    • an empty glass
  • Instructions:
    1. Take the cap off of the water bottle and cut off the bottom.
    2. Turn it upside-down and wrap the coffee filter around the mouth, securing it with the rubber band.
    3. Add a layer of gravel, then the sand, then the fine sand. Do this from the bottom of the bottle.
    4. Place the water bottle (still upside down) in the empty glass, then pour the dirty water through the bottom of the water bottle.
    5. Watch as the water filters through the different layers, ending with clear water at the bottom.
